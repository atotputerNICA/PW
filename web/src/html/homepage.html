ABOUT US

Nature plays a large part in the floral designing process and here we produce all of our events. Our designs are inspired by our gardens and nature, they possess movement and freedom as they do in the gardens. Our bouquets are untamed and display an organic array of seasonal textures. We have a strong belief in sourcing local, organic, fresh product when possible and grow here on the farm as well. Our sustainable practices reflect our love of nature.
